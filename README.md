# Brightlight-Coffee-shop-project
Overview of the Case Study

This case study analyzes sales data from Bright Coffee Shop to understand business performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends across products, time periods, and store locations.

Methodology (How the Case Study Was Done)

The analysis was conducted using Databricks SQL to query and transform the data and Microsoft Excel to create pivot tables and visualizations. Revenue was calculated and analyzed by month, week, day, time classification, store location, and product categories, types, and details.

Key Insights (What Was Found)

Sales are higher during weekdays and mid-mornings, with June and Week 4 showing peak performance. Coffee and Barista Espresso are the main revenue drivers and Hell’s Kitchen is the top-performing store. Lower sales were observed during weekends, nights and Week 1, as well as in some products and locations.

Summary

The study identified key sales patterns and performance drivers, highlighting opportunities to improve low-performing periods, products, and locations. The analysis supports better decision-making to increase overall revenue.
